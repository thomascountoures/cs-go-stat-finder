App ideas/overview

- App which shows team and player statistics from CSGO teams
- Can view individual teams by clicking on them from a list view
	- Individual team has a list of players
		- Can click on individual players to see individual stats
			- Individual stats: Player name, Country, Kills, Time Played, Accuracy, Headshot%, Win %
- Individual teams stats:
	- Tournament Wins, Tournament Losses, Win %
